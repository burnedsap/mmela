Welcome to Mann Mela

“Mann Mela” is a museum of young people’s mental health stories from India.

Each story is told using an art and technology-assisted exhibit, enabling you to interact with their world as they travel through their journey of finding hope, identity, meaning and empowerment.

Through sharing people’s real experiences, Mann Mela tackles common misconceptions about mental health and mental ilness in order to break down stigma and discrimination and encourage help-seeking.
